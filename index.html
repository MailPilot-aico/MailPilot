Frequently asked questions

How secure is my data?

Your text is processed solely to create your email, over an encrypted connection, and is never shared for advertising or training. API keys and credentials stay protected on the server.

How many devices does a license cover?

The Starter plan covers one workstation (1 PC). With the Business plan your team uses the copilot on multiple devices at once — centrally managed.

What are the cancellation terms?

All plans can be cancelled monthly, with no minimum term. You can cancel any time to the end of the billing period — your access stays active until then.
